Hand portion sizing is a visual, tool-free method for estimating serving sizes during dietary adherence. By using the individual's own hand dimensions, it automatically scales portions to body size and eliminates the need for digital tracking or food scales.

## Visual Measurement Standards
| Nutrient Category | Visual Guide | Approximate Weight/Volume |
|---|---|---|
| **Protein** | 1 Palm | 85-110g (cooked meat) |
| **Carbohydrates** | 1 Fist (or soap-sized slice) | 75g cooked rice / 40g bun / 35g bread |
| **Vegetables/Fruit** | 1 Fist or handful | ~1 cup leafy / 1 root veg / 1 apple |
| **Fats/Sauces** | 1 Thumb/Finger tip | Minimize; high caloric density |

## Application Guidelines
- Apply per meal to maintain consistent daily macros
- Adjust based on activity level: scale up for high training days, scale down for rest days
- Prioritize accuracy for high-density foods (oils, nuts, dressings) by strictly adhering to the thumb limit
- Simplifies adherence in social or dining-out scenarios
