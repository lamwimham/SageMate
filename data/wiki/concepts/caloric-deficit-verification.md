Caloric deficit verification refers to practical, physiological indicators that confirm a dietary approach is successfully creating the energy imbalance required for fat loss. It shifts focus from strict mathematical tracking to observable biofeedback.

## Primary Indicators
1. **Pre-Sleep Hunger**: Experiencing mild, manageable hunger before bed suggests daily energy intake is below maintenance.
2. **Weekly Weight Trend**: Consistent decline in morning, fasted body weight (averaged weekly). Note: Menstrual cycle water retention may temporarily mask fat loss; ignore single-week fluctuations during this phase.
3. **Portion Adherence**: Strictly following recommended hand-based serving sizes naturally induces a deficit for most sedentary to moderately active individuals.

## Implementation Strategy
- Weigh daily under identical conditions (fasted, post-bathroom)
- Calculate a rolling 7-day average to smooth daily variance
- Correlate weight trend with hunger signals to adjust portion sizes incrementally
- Avoid drastic calorie cuts; aim for a sustainable 10-20% deficit to preserve metabolic rate and lean mass
