MCP clients are front-end applications or AI interfaces that connect to one or more MCP servers. They serve as the orchestration layer, requesting resources, passing user prompts, and invoking tools exposed by servers to enable seamless interaction between humans, AI models, and external systems.

## Supported Platforms
The open standard is natively supported across major AI assistants (e.g., Claude, ChatGPT) and integrated development environments (e.g., Visual Studio Code, Cursor, MCPJam), enabling consistent user experiences across different software.
