Anti-inflammatory antioxidant foods are dietary components rich in phytochemicals and vitamins that neutralize free radicals and reduce systemic inflammation. They support metabolic health by improving insulin sensitivity, regulating blood sugar, and curbing cravings, making them highly effective for weight management.

## ORAC Scoring System
Oxygen Radical Absorbance Capacity (ORAC) quantifies a food's ability to neutralize free radicals.
- **Good**: ORAC > 1,000
- **Excellent**: ORAC > 5,000
- **Elite/King Tier**: ORAC > 10,000

## Metabolic Mechanisms
- Reduces oxidative stress and chronic low-grade inflammation
- Enhances insulin sensitivity and glucose regulation
- Modulates hunger hormones, preventing overconsumption
- Supports efficient cellular metabolism during caloric restriction
