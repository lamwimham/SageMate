The MCP ecosystem is the open-standard network of compliant clients, servers, tools, and platforms that interoperate using the Model Context Protocol. It establishes a "build once, integrate everywhere" development paradigm, eliminating vendor lock-in and custom integration overhead.

## Value by Stakeholder
- **Developers:** Significantly reduces the time and complexity of building or integrating AI capabilities.
- **AI Applications/Agents:** Gains instant access to a growing, shared library of data sources and actionable tools.
- **End-Users:** Benefits from more capable, context-aware AI assistants that can securely retrieve personal or organizational data and perform autonomous actions.
