# Yoga Routine

## 20-Minute Morning Flow

1. **Child's Pose** — 1min, breathe deeply
2. **Cat-Cow** — 10 rounds
3. **Downward Dog** — 5 breaths
4. **Sun Salutation A** — 3 rounds
5. **Warrior I & II** — 5 breaths each side
6. **Triangle Pose** — 3 breaths each side
7. **Seated Forward Fold** — 1min
8. **Supine Twist** — 1min each side
9. **Savasana** — 2min

## Recovery Day (Rest Day)

- 45min yin yoga
- Focus on hips and lower back
- Use bolsters and blocks
