## Evening

Watched a talk by [[Andy Matuschak]] on "Evergreen notes". Key insight: notes should be **atomic** and **concept-oriented**, not source-oriented.

This changes how I think about the SageMate compiler. Instead of one note per source, we should extract **concepts** and link back to sources.
