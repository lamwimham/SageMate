# Investment Research

Quantitative trading research and portfolio management.

## Philosophy

Inspired by [[WorldQuant]] and [[Two Sigma]] — systematic, data-driven, diversified.

> "An alpha is a combination of mathematical expressions, computer source code, and configuration parameters that can be used, in combination with historical data, to make predictions about future movements of various financial instruments."
> — [[Tulchinsky et al, 2015]]

## Strategy Categories

| Category | Horizon | Examples |

## Research Pipeline

1. **Idea Generation** — Screeners, academic papers, alternative data
2. **Backtesting** — [[Backtrader]] + custom framework
3. **Risk Analysis** — Drawdown, Sharpe, max correlation
4. **Paper Trading** — 3-month validation
5. **Live Deployment** — Position sizing via [[Kelly Criterion]]
