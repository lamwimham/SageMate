# Kakushadze and Tulchinsky, 2015

**Title**: *Some Empirical Properties of Real-Life Quantitative Trading Alphas*  
**Authors**: Zura Kakushadze, Igor Tulchinsky  
**Year**: 2015

## Summary

Study of 4,000 real-life alphas from WorldQuant. Found:
- Returns strongly correlated with volatility
- No significant dependence on turnover
- Pair-wise correlations are low (~15%)

## Key Equations

Returns scale with volatility:

$$R \sim \sigma^{\eta}$$

Where:
- $R$ = alpha return
- $\sigma$ = realized volatility
- $\eta \approx 0.76$ (empirical)

## Methodology

1. Data: proprietary WorldQuant alpha data
2. Metrics: Sharpe ratio, turnover, cents-per-share
3. Analysis: sample covariance matrix, correlation matrices
