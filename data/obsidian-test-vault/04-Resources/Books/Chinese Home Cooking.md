# Chinese Home Cooking


**Author**: Fuchsia Dunlop  
**Rating**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

## Favorite Recipes

### [[Mapo Tofu]]

- Silken tofu
- Doubanjiang (fermented bean paste)
- Sichuan peppercorns
- Ground pork (optional)

Key technique: **"Red oil"** (红油) — slowly fry doubanjiang until oil turns red.

### Kung Pao Chicken

- Chicken thigh, cubed
- Dried red chilies
- Sichuan peppercorns
- Peanuts (fried separately)

Key technique: **Velveting** — marinate chicken with cornstarch and oil for 15min.

### Dry-Fried Green Beans

- Long beans, blistered in hot oil
- Minced pork
- Preserved mustard greens (芽菜)
