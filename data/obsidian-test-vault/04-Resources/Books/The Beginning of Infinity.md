# The Beginning of Infinity

**Author**: David Deutsch  
**Published**: 2011  
**Status**: Reading (Part I done)  
**Rating**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

## Thesis

Explanatory knowledge is the only thing that matters for progress. Everything else (resources, wealth, technology) follows from it.

## Key Arguments

### Against Inductivism

You cannot derive general theories from specific observations. All scientific theories are **conjectures** subject to criticism.

> "Observations are theory-laden."

### Explanatory Knowledge Has Universal Reach

Good explanations apply everywhere:
- Laws of physics → apply across the universe
- Computer science → applies to any computation
- Epistemology → applies to any knowledge system

### The Beginning of Infinity

We are at the beginning of an infinite journey of knowledge creation. There are no fundamental limits to what we can understand.
