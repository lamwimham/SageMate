# PKM (Personal Knowledge Management)

The practice of capturing, organizing, and retrieving personal knowledge to support thinking, learning, and creativity.

## Core Principles

1. **Capture everything** — ideas, quotes, observations
2. **Organize for retrieval** — not just storage
3. **Distill over time** — literature → permanent notes
4. **Express publicly** — writing is thinking
