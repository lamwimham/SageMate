# David Deutsch

Pioneer of quantum computing, philosopher of science.

## Contributions

- **Quantum Computing**: Proposed quantum Turing machine (1985)
- **Quantum Algorithm**: Deutsch-Jozsa algorithm
- **Epistemology**: [[The Beginning of Infinity]], [[The Fabric of Reality]]

## Key Ideas

1. **Explanatory Knowledge** — the only thing that enables progress
2. **Fallibilism** — all knowledge is conjectural
3. **Many-Worlds Interpretation** — quantum mechanics
